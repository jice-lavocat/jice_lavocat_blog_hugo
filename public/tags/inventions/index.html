50 inventions stories

I just found an interesting book in Library I was working in. The book is called “Why didn’t I think of that?” and is written by A. Freeman and B. Golden. Inside you will discover the story of 50 inventions that ‘changed’ our society. This book may contain some unknown product for non-US people (like Cuisinart,Frito-Lay, Jello …) but most of them are known worldwide. You will discover for instance that the yoyo come from the Philippines, that the post-it was not first believed to be useful for 3M … The most important aspect of this book (apart from entertaining and culture) is for entrepreneur, managers and inventors. »
